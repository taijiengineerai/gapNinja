gapNinja closes the gap between your resume and the job you actually want. Paste any job description and it scores how well you fit — even when you're not a perfect match — shows exactly which skills are missing, and drafts a tailored cover letter and follow‑up email for you. Spend your time landing interviews, not firing off applications that bounce off a resume filter.

No public feed, no spam — gapNinja only ever spreads the old-fashioned way: one friend, one email, one invite at a time.
