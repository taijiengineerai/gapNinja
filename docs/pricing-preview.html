Questions

What happens to my data if I go over 15 applications on Free?

Nothing is deleted. You'll keep full access to your 15 most recent applications; older ones are archived and become read-only until you upgrade.

Are job comparisons and skills-gap scans really unlimited on Free?

Yes — compare your resume against as many job descriptions as you want, anytime, on any plan. The 15-application limit only applies to how many results you keep saved in your tracker. Running a comparison never counts against it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings whenever you like — you'll keep Pro access through the end of your current billing period.

Is there a discount for students or career changers?

Reach out through Support and we'll take care of you.
